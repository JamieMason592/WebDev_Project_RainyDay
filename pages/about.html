About Anime Haven

Welcome to Anime Haven! A storefront and blog dealing with all things anime and manga! Discuss the latest series and check out any merch you might want to pick up! I hope to foster a community of like-minded users with the same passion for anime and manga as me, where everyone can share and discuss their favourite media.

About the Owner

Hola! My name is Sofia Martinez and I'm the owner of Anime Haven. I've been a huge anime fan for years and want to share my passion with others! I want this site to be all about fun and fandom, with something for everyone. If you love anime as much as I do, I am sure there is something for you on Anime Haven!
